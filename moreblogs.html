Want To Learn More?

  1. Apps for Guided Meditation
    • Headspace – Great for beginners with easy-to-follow guided sessions.
    • Calm – Offers sleep stories, breathing exercises, and relaxing music.
    • Insight Timer – Free access to thousands of meditation sessions.

  2. YouTube Channels for Meditation
    • The Honest Guys – Calming guided meditations.
    • Yoga with Adriene – Yoga & mindfulness practices.
    • Great Meditation – Short and effective meditation exercises.

  3. Books on Meditation & Mindfulness
    • The Miracle of Mindfulness by Thích Nhất Hạnh – A classic on mindfulness practices.
    • Wherever You Go, There You Are by Jon Kabat-Zinn – A deep dive into mindful living.
    • 10% Happier by Dan Harris – A journalist’s journey into meditation and its benefits.

  4. Podcasts on Meditation & Mindfulness
    • Daily Meditation Podcast – Short, daily meditation sessions.
    • Sadhguru Guided Meditation – Traditional guided meditation practices.
    • Untangle by Meditation Studio – Insights from experts on meditation and mental wellness.

  5. Websites & Online Communities
    • Mindful.org – Articles, practices, and courses on mindfulness.
    • The Chopra Center – Meditation techniques and well-being resources.
    • Reddit - r/Meditation – A community for sharing meditation experiences.
